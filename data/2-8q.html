What Order is defined here?

  • This order results from combining or comparing systems of relations.
  • Several systems are coordinated:
    • “contracts and promises are articulations of a unique human quality…
    • mutual trust, which coordinates human relations”:
      • Here, contracts and promises are seen as the instantiation of a broader principle coordinating human interactions.
  • Words occur like:
    • Autonomy
    • Parallelism
    • Heteronomy
    • Completeness
    • Incompleteness
  • Such words “build in” a coordination of systems.
