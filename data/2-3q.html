What Order is defined here?

  • A label given a group of concrete things (e.g., the label “furniture” instead of listing objects “desks, chairs, tables”).
  • A label “feelings” instead of listing experiences of “happy, sad, mad”.
  • Classification words like “furniture,” “feelings,” “group,” “the law,” “teachers,” “politicians,” “polluters” are used.
  • Dualisms (e.g., best/worst, good/bad) and stereotypes result from using label variables.
