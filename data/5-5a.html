Metasystematic (12)

  • Variables - Alternatives, effects, side-effects, taking no action.
  • Relations - 1. Relates the effects and side-effects of each treatment including taking no action. 2. Asks the Patient questions about the treatments making sure the Patient understands. 3. Makes sure patient is comfortable making a decision with the information. 4. Asks the Patient to think carefully before choosing a treatment.
  • Systems - Informing, consenting.
  • Relations among systems - Asks the patient to coordinate what they have both said about the alternatives to make a decision on treatment.
