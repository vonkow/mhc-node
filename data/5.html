  • The following 5 stories are set in another country. In each story, a Counselor tries to help a competent Patient to solve a given set of problems. The problems are serious. It is now time to choose a treatment to improve the situation. All the Counselors care for their Patients equally. All the Counselors highly recommend and provide the same basic treatments. But, the Counselors arrive at their choice of how to help in different ways. In every case, the problems worsen and those who are affected suffer. During a general review of these bad results, each Counselor\'s method of choosing a treatment was looked at. Below, the Counselors give accurate accounts of their usual talk with their Patients.
